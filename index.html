Methodology — Cost 1 (Analyst capacity): analysts × salary × (prep% − 15% unified baseline). Prep% source: Anaconda State of Data Science 2023 (45% avg). Unified baseline 15% from Amplitude customer benchmarks.   Cost 2 (Integration engineering tax): data engineers × salary × maintenance%. Maintenance% reflects time spent on pipeline upkeep, schema drift, and cross-tool reconciliation for N×(N−1)/2 integration pairs.   Cost 3 (Decision-lag tax): requests/wk × 52 × stakeholders × analyst hourly × lag days × 8hrs × 15% context-switch factor. 15% = interruption + re-briefing cost, not idle time (Gloria Mark, UCI).   Budget overhead ratio: total cost ÷ total analytics team budget (analysts + data engineers).   Experiment capacity: freed analyst hrs/yr ÷ 40hrs per experiment cycle (industry avg for instrumentation + analysis).
